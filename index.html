Core Areas of Focus

Built around disciplined methodology — not prediction, not gut feel. Each area reinforces the others within the framework.

📊
Volume Profile
LVN, HVN, VAH, VAL, and POC identification. Weekly and daily profile analysis for high-probability location mapping using Auction Market Theory principles.
🏗️
Market Structure
Multi-timeframe context analysis. 4H/Daily/Weekly directional bias identification with 15-minute execution confirmation. Structure-based stop placement only.
🕯️
Price Action
Engulfing and Pin Bar confirmation candles. VMA volume confirmation. Decisive rejection identification vs. controlled acceptance behavior on the 15M timeframe.
🛡️
Risk Management
Fixed 1% risk per trade. Minimum 1:3 R:R. Break-even at 1:1. Weekly loss limit enforcement. 2–3 trade maximum weekly exposure. Capital preservation first.
🧠
Trading Psychology
Mechanical execution over deliberative decision-making. Emotional state journaling. Systematic review process. Separating process quality from individual trade outcomes.
🏦
Prop Firm Trading
Challenge and evaluation compliance. Drawdown management. Daily and weekly loss limit protocols. Funded account preservation strategies with institutional risk discipline.
📈
Journal & Analytics
Model-specific performance tracking across LVN Rejection, VA Rotation, and Value Acceptance. Mistake classification system. Edge evaluated over 20–30 trade statistical samples.
